About Us

The UNL Operating Systems & Open-Source Group (OS2G) is a club for users of Linux and other Free and Open-Source Software (FOSS) to meet, socialize, and learn from one another.

We host events to introduce new members to the world of FOSS and enhance their computer literacy, covering a wide range of topics including server administration, data science, cybersecurity, computer hardware, and programming languages.

Interested? Attend one of our in-person meetings, or reach out to us online via Discord or NvolveU!
